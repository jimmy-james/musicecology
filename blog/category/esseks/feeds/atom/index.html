Astronaut School Tour featuring <a href="https://www.facebook.com/freddytoddmusic" target="_blank">Freddy Todd</a> and <a href="https://www.facebook.com/spacejesusmusic" target="_blank">Space Jesus</a> hits <a href="https://www.facebook.com/events/1552690481615319/" target="_blank">Portland, Maine</a> tonight, and <a href="https://www.facebook.com/events/387734498060390" target="_blank">Boston</a> on Saturday, and both nights will feature art from <a href="https://www.facebook.com/WelchBrothersArt" target="_blank">The Welch Brothers</a>. These are must see shows because Freddy Todd, Spacey J, and The Welch Brothers, but there's MORE - tonight in Portland <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ZebblerStudios">Zebbler</a> is in the house with <a href="https://www.facebook.com/visurreal" target="_blank">VISURREAL</a> on the stage design, and the support lineup is no joke either - <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Supersillyus" target="_blank">Supersillyus</a>, <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Esseks" target="_blank">Esseks</a>, and<a href="https://www.facebook.com/LevitationJones" target="_blank"> Levitation Jones</a>. If you don't know these acts now is the time to check them out. Asylum in Portland is a little roomier and houses 8 dual 18" subs - the sound and visuals are gonna be massive - Middle East should be a good ol fashioned dirty water basement rager. No need to choose, go to both shows... and then catch them again next week in <a href="https://www.facebook.com/events/595984933836020/" target="_blank">Providence</a> ;)</p>
