If memory serves, we booked one of, if not the first lespecial show in Boston, and since then we've seen this band develop and build a loyal cult following that's been rapidly expanding for quite some time. This summer has seen lespecial building momentum with a number of high profile festival sets and a handful of releases that have boasted exceptionally high production standards.</p> <p>This week lespecial treats us to not only a live video of their song "Squid Rising"&nbsp;(which looks and sounds great) from infinity Music Hall in Norfolk, CT, but also an 8 bit remix of their tune "Dangus Khan" created by Scott Hannay of Mister F.</p> <p>This weekend you can catch lespecial playing Wild Woods Festival in Croyden, NH on Friday (Aug. 8) and (EDIT)&nbsp;<del>MazFest</del>&nbsp;Return to the Roots Festival in Downsville, NY on Saturday (Aug. 9).
