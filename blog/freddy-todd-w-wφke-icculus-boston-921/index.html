Freddy Todd hits Middle East Upstairs tomorrow night with support from locals WΦKE & Icculus.

At this point Freddy Todd is OG in Glitch Hop/Bass Music communities - this is a great opportunity to catch him in a smaller room on a weekend, especially for the 18+ crowd. Check out his new Chrondor EP here!

WΦKE is the latest from VII Studios, expect to hear some freshie original tunes and unreleased biz to keep things interesting.

Word up to Daily Special for a solid lineup top to bottom!

Tickets ($15 adv)
