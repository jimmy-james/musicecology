Ali Berger

Ali Berger makes funk music with drum machines. He's played lots of Boston venues, including the Middle East, the Sinclair, Wonder Bar, Good Life, RISE, All Asia, and the Phoenix Landing. For this particular show, Ali is bringing his computer and an unprecedented arsenal of hardware to play a free-form, highly improvised live PA set with lots of brand-new, exclusively-live grooves. Prepare for distorted acid basslines and raw drum machine beats.

http://www.facebook.com/alibergermakesmusic

http://twitter.com/abergermd

http://alibergermusic.com/

Dusty Digital

Dusty Digital is a dj/producer, remixer from Boston. Dustin's melodic style will put you in a trance seeing neon lights and disco balls. With genres ranging from Progressive house, 90's dance, Italo, Electro, Trance and Nu-disco.

http://www.facebook.com/Dustydigital86

10 PM - 1:45 AM, 21+, $5
