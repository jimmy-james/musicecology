AxH
AxH, (formerly Grizzly), has been dedicated to bass music and DJ-ing for over a decade. Based in Boston, MA, AxH got his start as Prodigal Son, producing Ragga Jungle that received praise and plays all over the world. AxH caught the Dubstep bug in 2006 while serving overseas in the U.S. Military, and has been pushing forward ever since! He has been releasing music digitally and on wax since 2004, and has played coast to coast in North America, supporting his releases. 2008 saw AxH perform in Kiev, Ukraine, a set that people are still talking about today.
He has become a prolific producer as he's honed his deep, dark, and heavy style. His debut Dubstep album is due out on Brooklyn based TUBA Records this spring. AxH is one of the residents for BASSIC Boston, (Boston's premiere Dubstep night), where he can be heard dropping entire sets of his original tracks. You can also hear him on BASSIC's Sub.FM show every Saturday night. Check out his material at

http://www.mixcloud.com/AxH

Steppo
With over ten years experience in the game, and a serious love for all things music, DJ Steppo has rocekd crowds from Vermont to Miami and San Francisco, and has played along such acts as Pendulum, Adam F, Mayhem, Juju, Reid Speed and many more. He recently started his own label, Lovewave Music - focusing on the futuristic sounds of dubstep, house, and techno. you can catch his radio show on dubstep.fm every 2nd and 4th wednesday of the month.


A$hley
It all began for A$hley in 2005 as she embarked on her DJing career spinning jungle/drum & bass; soon after, she joined with the crew RI-DNB and began playing out at events in and around the New England area. Presently, A$hley can be found performing on any given weekend at various rave events, clubs, weeklies and radio shows all over the Northeast US. Over the years, A$hley has adopted many other genre styles into her sets which include but are not limited to dubstep, drumstep/d&b, breaks, house, and downtempo. In 2009, A$hley joined with the infamous Tight Crew as a DJ/decorator. Since then, she has gone on to also represent for True Crew,761 Productions, LTD Unltd, and NYCravers and is currently a resident at Boston's newest Dubstep Sunday night weekly "The Drop". A$hley is also a Content Manager and Curator of Dubstep for Satellite Record's recently launched digital music site which features her very own monthly Top 10 DJ chart.
