Our friend Bakir is back from NY again! This time he's brought 2 more of his Tuba crew along! 
May 28th @ Wonder Bar

21+ with ID, 10 PM - 1:45 AM, $5

Bakie and Sirois- LIVE! (Tuba-NYC)

Bakir and Sirois (of the Spit Brothers) have been lighting up dance floors coast to coast with their set of electrifying live bass music since 2008. Combining heavy doses of sub bass with a blend of electronic and acoustic drums, their selection ranges from deep reggae dubstep to intense dance floor smashers. Both musicians have played extensively around the country; Brent drumming for Mighty Mystic, has shared bills with the likes of Barrington Levy, Steel Pulse, Movado, and the Wailers. Bakir and the Spit Brothers have been on lineups alongside Caspa and Rusko, Tinie Tempah, Eskmo, Daedalus, Kromestar, Jazzsteppa, and many others.

Bakir has played trumpet on tour with the live London/Berlin dubstep band Jazzsteppa both in the United States and Europe. Living previously in San Francisco, Bakir and The Spit Brothers were named one of SF Weekly's 2010 dubstep ambassadors.

They are currently based in Brooklyn, NY, where Bakir runs the Dubs Alive label and TUBA, his vinyl imprint. Check out http://www.soundcloud.com/dubsalive for free Spit Brothers mixes and tracks.
