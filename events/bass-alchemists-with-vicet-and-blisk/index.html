The Bass Alchemists are Quint Sixx and Lopht

The duo is comprised of electronic music producers/DJs Alex Kaplan and Cameron Merrick. Originally from London, Alex is now living in Boston and trying to bring more love for bass heavy music across the Atlantic. He has been immersed in the bass music scene all over the world including Hong Kong, London, California, and the North East. Over the last few years, Cameron has played an arr...ay of events around Denver, including the notorious Beta Nightclub alongside producers such as DJ Fei Fei, Paul Anthony, Str8house, Booklyn Bounce and more. After the two met, the 'Bass Alchemists' were born - playing alongside acts at sold out shows including: 'DATSIK', 'Zeds Dead', 'Terravita'l, 'Breakage' and 'Jack Beats'.

Now signed to Base Bookings, Bass Alchemists plan to deliver more heavy basslines throughout Boston and the rest of the US...
