Billy Brown

"The only person you should make music for is yourself. Only then do you deliver true expression."

Hailing from Boston, Billy Brown is a producer and DJ of various flavors of bass. With a degree in Biotechnology from the illustrious Worcester Polytechnic Institute, Billy Brown's love of science has shifted from the biological to the digital sounds of electronic music. Deep, sensual, tropical, dirty, gangster, and booty breaking are just some adjectives that describe the basslines heard on the musical journey one will experience.

http://www.facebook.com/whoisBillyBrown
