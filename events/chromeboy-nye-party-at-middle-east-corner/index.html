Dec. 31, 2012, 9 p.m. - Jan. 1, 2013, 2 a.m. at Middle East Corner

(Comments)

Chromeboy is Blue Boy Productions and half of Chroma Concept. A live band that is here to take over the universe.
Mike Clairmont on Bass
Chris Narainen on Drums
Ezra Platt on Guitar 
Alex Russo on Keys and Production

We started as an improv band that relied on spur of the moment interaction but now we got a bunch of original music for you in addition to the unexpected.

We will be throwing our own NYE party on December 31st and this thing is gonna pop off big time. We will take over the Middle East Corner til close! Sound by BBP.

9:30-10:30 - µSorcerer (Alex Russo) DJ set
10:45-11:05 - Turtle Truck *world debut* (featuring MC Happy Feet)
11:20 til CLOSE - CHROMEBOY

Only $10
Bring your party boots.
