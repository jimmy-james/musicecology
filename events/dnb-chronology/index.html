Jan. 26, 2010, 10 p.m. at Wonder Bar

(Comments)

The Drum & Bass music community in Boston has been much respected for its DJ and production talent over the years, always keeping it cutting-edge and dancefloor moving! Tuesday January 26 @ MUSIC ECOLOGY will bring together selectas both veteran and newcomer to provide your mind (and more importantly your dancin' feet!) with a dancefloor history lesson of epic proportions, taking you on a journey from the roots of Breakbeat Hardcore to the oven-fresh dubplates of the new decade and EVERYTHING in between! As you all must know by now, there has been a most tragic earthquake in Haiti resulting in many lives lost and many families with no food or shelter. ALL proceeds on the night will go to benefit these victims! Come shake it for a great cause and show how our unique community can give back! DJs on the night (spinning in a tag-team fashion) include: NUGGS (aka SCOTCH-1), PRODIGAL SON, DISKO, ASSEMBLER, BASEK, FURIOUS STYLES, more to be announced! Art display and sale by MUNSTER with proceeds also going to Haiti Earthquake Relief fund. Adopt a Munster and help a great cause. LIVE art display TBA Delicious edible treats concocted by our resident foodie Deven Smith. COME OUT! COME EARLY! COME DANCING!

Wonder Bar
186 Harvard Avenue
Allston, MA
02134-2806
