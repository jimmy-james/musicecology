July 7, 2012, 10 p.m. - July 8, 2012, 2 a.m. at Machine Nightclub

(Comments)

"Therefore I should infer from analogy that probably all the organic beings which have ever lived on this earth have descended from some one primordial form, into which life was first breathed." -Darwin

No matter where you're from, how old you are, or how much money you have we're all just evolved from a puddle of goo.
Come Celebrate the width and breadth of live with us!!!

Deco by the lovely Kate Van Rees

Mindblowing Visuals by Projected Reality Crew

Musical Guests for the Night to include:

Pressplay: Whether you've seen him play at such Boston Institutions as Elements and Bassic or if you were here the last time Pressplay tore Genesis a new one you know this man can rock a dance floor! Maybe you've never caught a set from this young up and comer if so you're in for a huge treat! Come find out why he gets so much respect from those who command it themselves... and yes he had the name way before the whole Deadmau5 affair.

CDubs: CO-Founder of BASSIC, Boston's longest running dubstep night, Grad Student, Human Canvas, and more.

with opening support from resident Professor Pious (Glitch.fm, Music Ecology)

Machine Nightclub (Fenway)
1254 Boylston Street in Boston
"Fenway" or "Kenmore" stops on the Green Line

10 PM - 2 AM, 21+, $10 - includes admission to Factory in the lounge

Music Ecology is a Boston based event production collective formed in the summer of 2009. Our weekly Tuesday night residency at Wonder Bar a well as numerous events throughout the city have exposed Boston audiences to numerous local, national and international talent. Our upcoming events include:

July 24th Music Ecology 3 year anniversary party
July 31st Shigeto at Music Ecology
Aug. 7th Sky Tree at Music Ecology
Aug. 28th Om Unit at Music Ecology
