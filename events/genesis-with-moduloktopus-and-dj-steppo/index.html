Moluloktopus
Makes boundless genres ranging from House, Breaks, Glitch, Juke, Experimental Dubstep, Chillout, Moombahton, Future Garage & many more varying hybrids. With this flexible Diversity,

The live shows offer all original music, a divine taste of zestful guest performers, and the ability to chameleon his way in and out of various electronic music scenarios.

With the prime goal of bridging technology with a vivid imagination, Dominican artist Wigbert Caro is on a mission to paradigm shift the protocosmic sounds of MODULOKTOPUS to modern times.
http://soundcloud.com/modulok
