    Come join two members of the Boston sonic soul tribe as they take flight with their brand new albums!!

(((

  • LIVING LIGHT )))

Brainchild of Psylab keyboardist Eartha Harris, 
Living Light blends downtempo beats with world percussion, psychedelic glitches, Indian vocals, middle eastern melodies, soothing electronic harmonies and deep pulsing dub baselines. The name has multiple meanings, referring to "living lightly" upon the planet, maintaining a positive outlook on life, and gives nod to the human body's cells emitting photons. We are all the stuff of stars ;)

Living Light's brand new full length album "Ecliptic Visions" will be available for purchase on Beatport and all major music outlets 11/25/2013

Soundcloud : http://soundcloud.com/livinglight
Facebook: http://facebook.com/livinglightfrequencies

  • HEISS )))

Heissmusik Frank Heiss is a Sonic Sorcerer. Using the medium of audio and practice of alchemy, he transmutes black magic into Light, representing a portal through which psychic energy may pass and channeling Divine power from the Kosmos as a conduit making the reaction possible.

Ascensio(N), the follow up to HEISS Awake(N) drops here:
