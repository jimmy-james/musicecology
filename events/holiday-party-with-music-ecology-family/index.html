Dec. 25, 2012, 10 p.m. - Dec. 26, 2012, 2 a.m. at Wonder Bar

(Comments)

Add to Google Calendar

Ok kids, here is the deal. On December 25th - Christmas Day (yes, we are not afraid to use the word Christmas in a sentence) we will gather at Wonder Bar like we usually do. We'll start the evening right by watching a wonderful Christmas movie filled with laughter and joy and holiday spirit and that movie just happens to be Die Hard. What can be more festive than the line "Now I have a machine gun. Ho Ho Ho!"?
If we have enough willing people in the joint by midnight, then we can play some musics and do what we generally do on Tuesday nights at Wonder Bar. 
Join us if your family is too far away to visit during the holidays or if you need an extra-stiff drink after hanging out with them all day. Extra cheers for wearing antlers or elf ears. :)
There will be no cover as in FREE so don't sit this one out and try to get there by midnight if you wanna party!
Ho Ho Ho!

Wonder Bar
186 Harvard Avenue, Allston
"Harvard Avenue" stop on Green Line B

10 PM - 2 AM, 21+, $ZERO

Music Ecology is a Boston based event production collective formed in the summer of 2009. Our weekly Tuesday night residency at Wonder Bar a well as numerous events throughout the city have exposed Boston audiences to numerous local, national and international talent. 

For this and future events please visit http://www.musicecologyboston.com/
or find us on Facebook at http://www.facebook.com/MusicEcology
or follow us on twitter at https://twitter.com/music_ecology
check out live recordings from our Tuesday nights athttp://www.soundcloud.com/music-ecology
