Come wish happy birthday to the man who feeds us delicious pancakes first and then helps us shed the extra pounds in his yoga classes - Mr. Billy Gamble!

Does Jeff Bujak even need an introduction at Music Ecology? One of our favorite artists returns to Wonder Bar and we are stoked to have him back!
Jeff Bujak
Massachusetts based performance duo 'Bujak' is Jeff Bujak (on the keys) and Jen Dulong (on the LED hula-hoop). Jeff Bujak is a solo experimental/progressive music producer from Massachusetts who creates instrumental songs centered around classical composition, progressive rock and dance music that dives deep in the waters of complex live electronica. Jen Dulong performs on stage alongside Jeff with an LED hula-hoop providing beautiful lighting and movement with a visual depth to solo musician. Bujak is carving a new path in the live-music scene one show at a time. It's something different.http://www.bujaklive.com/

Roo
Joseph Nicora aka Roo born in 1978, exploded his unique style of deep progressive house into the east coast rave scene back in '97. His impressive gigs include legendary parties such as the Boo events in NYC, the Tunnel and Lime Light. Roo, previously known as D.J. Mavrick, has touched on break beats, trance, glitch and even found his own style in dirty electro house keeping the vibe positive and all kinds of dirty. He is no stranger to summer music festivals. He's made appearances at New York Harvest Festival, Camp Bisco, Heads in Harmony and Camp Coldbrook. Keeping with his theme of constant progression, Roo now finds himself somewhere between deep house, tech, and dirty disco.
http://www.mixcloud.com/roo-nicora/
