Their electric live showcase has been cultivating a fast growing and loyal fan base.</strong></p> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p dir="ltr"><strong>Their third studio effort 'Redivider' was release on December 21st 2012. Opting to take a different route than recording in a studio, the band brought their own to the barn of the farm and produced it by themselves. Redivider displays a drastic evolution in the band's songwriting skills as well as marking the first time ever that the band has added vocals into songs.
