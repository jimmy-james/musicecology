Professorpious, Ali Berger and BASEK &quot;lerNd&quot;2011-09-09T00:00:00+00:00Polar Vortex/events/author/polina/http://musicecologyboston.com/events/all-stars-feat-professorpious-ali-berger-and-basek-lernd/<p><strong>CruTopia</strong>, in conjunction with <strong>The Stone Church Meeting House,</strong> is proud to welcome Music Ecology All-Stars ProfessorPious, Ali Berger and BASEK to "lerNd", a weekly electronic music spotlight. These cats have been tearing up down in Boston, and here's your chance to catch them in New Hampshire. Music Ecology is a weekly gathering of producers, DJs, musicians and artists focused on exposing live, original, and fresh electronic music and local art. The night is held every Tuesday at Wonder Bar in Allston. <br /><br /><strong>Professorpious</strong> -(glitch.fm, Music Ecology) <br /><br /><a href="http://soundcloud.com/professorpious" target="_blank">http://soundcloud.com/professorpious</a><br /> <br /> Professorpious has been hooked on electronic music since the late nineties where he was drawn to the frenetic sounds of DnB as a young man. He bought some turntables found a warehouse and threw his first parties in late 2000. A brief trip to London in 2001 and a visit to Fabric nightclub provided the catalyst to switch styles from DnB to Breaks. Then in 2005 Pious attended Burning Man and heard Dubstep and Glitch Hop for the first time and instantly knew this was the sound of the future. Today you can hear anyone or all of these styles in a Pious set.<br /> Currently Pious holds down residency at Lost In Bass every Monday on glitch.fm as well as at the Tuesday weekly Music Ecology.<br /><br /><br /><strong>Ali Berger</strong> - <a href="http://aliberger.bandcamp.com/" target="_blank">http://aliberger.bandcamp.com/</a><br /><br />*************3$ 21+, 5$ 18+***********DOORS @ 8****************</p>
