DJ Zoses
My interest in electronic music started when I first heard trance. Trance's progression and ability to evoke strong emotions inspired me to find more conscious altering tracks. Now, I have moved away from the trance scene into the ever evolving scenes of bass music. Whether it's dub, garage, D&B, deep house or glitch hop - all that matters to me is making people dance.
Through house parties, the festival scene and a booming music/bar scene in Boston I have been able to make my dreams a reality.
As a DJ, I focus on creating a musical journey through various genres of bass music, keeping the crowd on its toes. Each set is unique and tailor fitted to the night/venue. I treat DJ'ing as an art and only spin tracks that I love.

Mindstream
Mindstream is the musical vision of Boston based producer and bassist Chris Mack. Chris began experimenting with electronic music in Spring 2011 when by playing the bass guitar while DJing parties. Shortly after, Chris began producing original beats and improvising with them live on the bass and keyboard, and Mindstream was born. Mindstream music blends hard-hitting dance beats and atmospheric soundscapes with funk, rock, and blues oriented instrumental improvisation, creating a unique atmosphere that shares the sensibilities of both jam bands and electronic dance music. Mindstream live is a positive and psychedelic, yet heavy and industrial, constantly evolving dance party.

FOGES
These days, people want unique and organic. That's what Ben Fogel provides. Also known as FOGES, Ben has been composing electronic music since 2007. In May of 2012, Fogel earned a Bachelor of Science in Music Technology and Composition in Boston Ma. Over the past five years Fogel has worked to cultivate an organic sound. This sound has been drawn from a variety of influences ranging from traditional orchestral music, to the world of sound design for interactive media. Currently FOGES continues to compose electronic music and create new boundaries for music as an art form.
www.soundcloud.com/foges
