Phenology, an 18+ dance party at Middle East Upstairs

featuring:
Psylab (http://psylab.org/) *record release party*
Blue Boy Productions (http://blueboyproductions.net/)
Wobblesauce (http://wobblesauce.com/)
ProfessorPious (http://nerdk0r3.com/)

Come celebrate with us as Music Ecology hosts its first 18+ show at Middle East Upstairs on Friday, March 25! We have a few of Boston's best live EDM bands together under one roof for only $10! Show some love and we'll turn this into a recurring phenomenon!

$10, 18+, link to buy presale tickets coming soon!

Music Ecology has been creating a loyal following by bringing top notch EDM talent to Boston since July 2009 with a weekly Tuesday night residency at Wonder Bar in Allston. In that time we've brought acts including: Bluetech, Heyoka, Jazzsteppa, Debruit, the Great Mundane, Chronicles of the Landsquid, Mount Kimbie, Kilowatts, the Spit Brothers, and much more, as well as countless local producers and bands.
