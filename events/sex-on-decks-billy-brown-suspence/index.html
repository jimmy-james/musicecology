Sex on Decks
The alter ego of Dan Rourke, Drummer for the 2011 Motif Magazine Jam Band of the year Viral Sound. With flawless precision, Sex on Decks blends unique sounds with a wide array of musical styles to serenade your eardrums before blowing them out with some of the funkiest bass heavy tracks ever to hit the decks. Appealing to a diverse audience Dan produces and spins eclectic tracks ranging from Nu-Disco to Booty-Bass House. With Dan's high-energy performance, and diverse track selection, Sex on Deck's forges an intimate bond with the audience you must feel to believe.


Billy Brown (AKA BrownNoize)


Suspence
Hailing from Concord, MA, Spencer a.k.a. Suspence moved to Boston in 2007 and has since been heavily pursuing a career in music production and Djing. Having been influenced by a wide array of dance music and hip hop growing up, and having had an exciting last couple of years connecting & playing with other musicians such as Charles Feelgood, TJR, Sergio Santos, Tamer Malki, Salvatore Lograsso and Chas Bronz, his main focus has now largely come to rest in Deep House -- producing & spinning anything with "that seductive trademark and body drippin' grooves." Suspence is also associated with the East Coast House Mob which includes Jaminic, and Chas Bronz.


10 PM - 2 AM, 21+, $5
