The Drop! True Crew's weekly party 1-26-14

This week it is my pleasure to pay homage to one of the nights that helped revive the electronic music scene here in Boston, Music Ecology. Its been a crazy 5 years and we will certainly be on the lookout for any one offs sponsored by the Music Ecology team!

Featuring Music Ecology residents:

Professor Pious - Trap, Twerk, Bass 
(Glitch.fm, Music Ecology)
http://soundcloud.com/professorpious

Alex Russo - mixed genre
(Blue Boy Productions, Music Ecology)
http://www.soundcloud.com/alexrussotechnoset

The Mad Mauritian - dub / reggae 
(Blue Boy Productions, Music Ecology)
https://soundcloud.com/the-madmauritian/madsub2013-mmix

**Event Photography by Evan Smith Photography**

19+ $7 -- 21+ $5
