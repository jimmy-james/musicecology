July 25, 2010, 7 p.m. at Wonder Bar

(Comments)

Add to Google Calendar Add to Outlook/iCal

Visual Enlightenment is a group art show held at Wonder Bar on the last Sunday of every month. Each installment features original work from 8 local artists as well as live music and DJ set later in the evening. This is an early evening endeavor as it starts at around 7 PM . Wonder Bar's inexpensive but wonderfully delicious kitchen will be a treat for those of you who are looking for Sunday night dinner plans. Catch a live jazz ...band playing from 8 PM and until 10 PM and a DJ after that (details below).
This month's installment will feature work by:

Amie Larson (http://www.amieart.com/)
23 Riddles (see images below)
Heather Rein (see images below)
Arthur V. Commerce (http://arthurvcommerce.com/)
Linda Nieman (http://www.lindaniemann.blogspot.com/)
Katrina Peterson (http://katrinapeterson.com/)
Dave Kazukiewicz (http://viewmorepics.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=user.viewPicture&friendID=37629796&albumId=1453772)
Adam Harmon (http://www.adamharmon.nobullart.com/)

The live music portion of the evening will be a real treat. Coming for this show from southern New Hampshire, is a piano-bass-drums trio Jazz First with a 2 hour performance of straight-ahead jazz and blues standards, written primarily in the first half of the twentieth century. (http://www.jazzfirstmusic.com/)
Click this link to go directly to one of their demos.
http://www.jazzfirstmusic.com/audio/Current_Demos/JustInTime.mp3

DJ Whatever will be taking over the sound at 10 PM. Part of Boston based live IDM duo Blue Boy Productions (http://www.blueboyproductions.net/), DJ Whatever has built his fame on the electronic music scene with sets at Wonder Bar, Enormous Room, The Good Life, Harper's Ferry, TT The Bears, Middle East, Common Ground, Glitch.fm internet radio show (Lost in Bass) as well as a number of underground "secret location" venues. Part of Music Ecology crew (http://musicecologyboston.com/) DJ Whatever is sure to keep the late night crowd dancing.

This event is 21+ with valid ID. It is absolutely FREE to attend, so please stop by for a quick glance or stay for dinner, jazz and dancing.

Wonder Bar
186 Harvard Avenue
Allston, MA
02134-2806
