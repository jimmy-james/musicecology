Music Ecology presents: Winter Solstice celebration w/ Cathode Rays, Lenore, MTN and much more...

This will be an epic event and I'll tell you why...

Tuesday, December 21st, 2010 is the Winter Solstice, marking the perihelion, where the orbit of a planetary body is closest to the Sun, and in this case, the Earth is closest to the sun. This has astrological and cultural significance unto itself but it just so happens that on that ...same night, there is a full moon (8:15pm) and a Total Lunar Eclipse occurring, This is ***NO BULLSHIT*** A once in a lifetime opportunity to be a part of. The next time that this alignment occurs is a long time from now (60+ years). The energy of the evening will be very pulling, emotional, vibrant, loving (or not), but all in all, it is going to be a very special event and my understanding is that this alignment will not happen again in our lifetime so get it while ya can!
